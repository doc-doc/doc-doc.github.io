I am currently a final year PhD student (2018 Fall-Now) at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua. From Nov. 2021 to Apr. 2022, I worked as a research intern at Sea AI Lab (SAIL) and was jointly advised by Dr. Pan Zhou and Prof. Shuicheng Yan. I also get lots of help from Prof. Angela Yao in my PhD study. Prior to that, I received my M.Eng degree from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences at 2018 and B.Eng. degree from Sichuan University at 2015, respectively.

I am interested in exploring and developing such AI techniques that draws high-level inspiration from the feats of human mind, including its statistic learning, structural reasoning as well as cognitive capabilities. My PhD focuses on video visual relation learning and its application in VideoQA.
